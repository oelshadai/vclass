BS9 Business Studies Quiz
=========================

Subject: Business Studies
Level: BS9
Duration: 30 minutes
Total Marks: 10

Instructions:
- Read each question carefully
- Select the best answer from the options provided
- Each question carries 2 marks
- No negative marking

Questions:
--------------------------------------------------

Q1. What does SWOT analysis stand for? (2 marks)
Topic: Strategic Planning

   A) Sales, Work, Operations, Technology
   B) Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
   C) Systems, Workflow, Objectives, Targets
   D) Strategy, Work, Organization, Time

Correct Answer: B
--------------------------------------------------

Q2. What is the primary purpose of market research? (2 marks)
Topic: Marketing

   A) To increase sales
   B) To understand customer needs
   C) To reduce costs
   D) To hire employees

Correct Answer: B
--------------------------------------------------

Q3. Which is NOT a method of market segmentation? (2 marks)
Topic: Marketing

   A) Geographic
   B) Demographic
   C) Psychographic
   D) Economic

Correct Answer: D
--------------------------------------------------

Q4. Which is an example of external recruitment? (2 marks)
Topic: Human Resources

   A) Promoting existing staff
   B) Job advertisements
   C) Internal transfers
   D) Staff recommendations

Correct Answer: B
--------------------------------------------------

Q5. Which of the following is NOT a factor of production? (2 marks)
Topic: Economics

   A) Land
   B) Labor
   C) Capital
   D) Profit

Correct Answer: D
--------------------------------------------------
